About us

We have a passion for hand-knitting and crocheting quality cat sweaters for cats of all sizes and activity levels. We are proud to say that our sweaters are tested and loved by indoor and outdoor cats alike.

Our Tough Serval cat sweaters are build to withstand even the most adventurous cat activites. Whether your kitty likes to lounge around the house or explore the outdoors, our sweaters will keep them warm while they do it.

If your cat manages to claw through a Tough Serval sweater, send it back with your reciept and we'll replace it via our lifetime warranty!

As a small business, we pride ourselves on our small batches, hand-checked for defects.
